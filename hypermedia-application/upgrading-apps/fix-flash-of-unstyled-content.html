Fix Flash of Unstyled Content

To fix this on these apps first add the following nested inside the <head> element at the top. Be sure to change the background-color to your theme's background color.

<style>html{visibility: hidden;opacity:0;background-color:#222;}</style>

Then above the line where you first load the javascript add the following

<style>html{visibility: visible;opacity: 1;}</style>

It should be right above

<asset-bundle src="~/bundle.min.js"></asset-bundle>

The page will now load to your background color, then switch over to a nicer view once everything is loaded.
